Detailed Description

This is a template for your AI. Everywhere, where TODO adpat appears, you have to insert your AIname instead of templateAI. For explanation of the methods, look into the AI-class documentation. Also you have to add this headerfile into the include-list in the file "hdr/ai/AIList.h".
