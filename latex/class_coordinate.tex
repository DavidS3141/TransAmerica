\subsection{Detailed Description}
Objects of this class are coordiantes on the board, where the x-\/axis is parallel to the west-\/east direction and the y-\/axis parallel to the north-\/(south-\/west) direction. The (0,0) coordinate is the most upper left corner of the board. The range of the x-\/values is from 0 to M\-A\-X\-\_\-\-X-\/1 and the y-\/values from 0 to M\-A\-X\-\_\-\-Y-\/1.
